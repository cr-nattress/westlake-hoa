# Story 10.7: Communication Best Practices Guide

## User Story

**As a** homeowner needing to communicate with the HOA
**I want** guidance on effective communication strategies
**So that** my concerns are properly documented and more likely to receive a response

## Description

Create a page or section that provides practical guidance for communicating with Bold Property Management and the HOA. Based on documented communication patterns, this guide helps homeowners communicate effectively and protect their interests.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Clear guidance on which email to use for different purposes
- [ ] Tips for effective communication (documentation, follow-up)
- [ ] Escalation triggers explained (when to escalate beyond management)
- [ ] Template or checklist for formal communications
- [ ] Warning about observed communication patterns (delayed responses)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly format
- [ ] Accessible

### Content Sections

#### 1. Choosing the Right Contact Method

| Situation | Contact | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| General questions | clientservices@boldsolutions.net | Best for initial inquiries |
| Maintenance requests | clientservices@boldsolutions.net | Document with photos |
| Records requests | Written request to Bold | Keep copy for records |
| Escalated matters | Board President or legal counsel | After management non-response |
| Emergency | AppFolio or posted emergency contact | Check community postings |

#### 4. Communication Pattern Warning

Based on documented patterns:
- Bulk notices via AppFolio email
- Limited responsiveness to individual follow-ups
- Escalation to legal counsel for disputes or accommodations
- Delayed or absent responses to owner emails
