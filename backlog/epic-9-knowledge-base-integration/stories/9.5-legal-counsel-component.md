# Story 9.5: Legal Counsel Information Component

## User Story

**As a** homeowner dealing with escalated matters
**I want** to know about the HOA's legal counsel
**So that** I understand when and why communications go through attorneys

## Description

Create a component displaying legal counsel information including the law firm, known attorney, their role, and important observations about how legal escalation works in practice.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Law firm name displayed: Alpenglow Law, LLC
- [ ] Attorney name shown: T.J. Voboril, Esq.
- [ ] Role/responsibilities listed
- [ ] Key observations noted (escalation patterns)
- [ ] Neutral, informational tone maintained
- [ ] Component reusable for /contacts page
