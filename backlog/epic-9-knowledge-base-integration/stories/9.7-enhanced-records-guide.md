# Story 9.7: Enhanced Records Request Guide

## User Story

**As a** homeowner wanting to request HOA records
**I want** clear information about my CCIOA rights and what to expect
**So that** I can effectively exercise my right to inspect records

## Description

Enhance the existing /records page with more specific information from the Knowledge Base, including what owners are entitled to, what's restricted, and importantly, what the "observed reality" has been versus the legal requirements.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Complete list of records owners are entitled to
- [ ] Clear list of restricted records (attorney-client, executive session, personal data)
- [ ] CCIOA 10-day response requirement highlighted
- [ ] "Observed Reality" section documenting actual experiences
- [ ] Practical tips based on documented patterns
- [ ] Request template or guidance (if not already present)
- [ ] Links to relevant policies
