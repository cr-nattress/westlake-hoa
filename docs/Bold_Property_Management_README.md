## Delegated Authority (per HOA Governance Policies)

Bold is designated as the **“Manager”** and is authorized to:

- Act as the HOA’s managing agent
- Receive and respond to owner communications
- Maintain official HOA records
- Coordinate records inspections
- Send delinquency notices (First Contact / Notice of Delinquency)
- Sign Notices of Assessment Lien (delegated authority)
- Maintain contact and communication logs

**Limitations:**
- Cannot independently initiate legal action
- Cannot refer accounts to attorneys without a recorded Board vote
- Cannot approve foreclosures

---

## Records & Compliance Responsibilities

Bold is the default custodian of Association records, including:
- Financial records
- Owner ledgers
- Contracts
- Insurance documentation
- Board communications (non-privileged)

Required to:
- Provide records within statutory timelines
- Coordinate inspections during normal business hours
- Track and document owner communications

---

## Insurance & Risk Administration

Bold is listed as the “care of” entity for HOA insurance materials, including:
- Master policy documentation
- Certificates of insurance
- Unit owner insurance notices

Bold is included under fidelity coverage as property manager.

## Notes

Bold is not a passive administrator. Under governing documents and Colorado law, it functions as an operational extension of the HOA and carries compliance, records, and enforcement responsibilities.

Delays, non-responses, or failures to act fall within this delegated role.
